About us

Making study fun again

Our Mission:

Study decoder was created to help students navigate the often confusing world of school syllabuses. Simplifying the syllabuses gives the student a competitive advantage as it allows the student to understand what they will learn and be assessed on.

Why We Exist:

School is stressful and competitive, but finding the right information shouldn't be. Study Decoder is designed to save students time, reduce confusion, and make studying a little less overwhelming, giving them a competitive advantage.
