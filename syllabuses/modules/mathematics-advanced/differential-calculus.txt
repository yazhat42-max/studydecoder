=== Differential calculus ===
Subject: mathematics-advanced
============================================================

In differential calculus, the idea of ‘gradient as a rate of change’ is used to find the rates of change of functions with non-constant rates of change. These functions are represented geometrically by curves. The process of finding these rates of change is known as differentiation. This process is of fundamental importance in mathematics and has applications in all quantitative fields of study, including physics, chemistry, medicine, engineering, computing, statistics, business, finance and economics.
