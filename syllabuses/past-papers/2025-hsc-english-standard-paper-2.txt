20 marks
Attempt Question 1Allow about 40 minutes for this section
Answer the question on pages 2–8 of the Paper 2 Writing Booklet. Extra writing booklets are 
available.
Your answer will be assessed on how well you:
● demonstrate understanding of how ideas about language, identity and culture are 
expressed through texts
● demonstrate understanding of how language is used to shape meaning about 
individuals and/or cultural groups 
● organise, develop and express ideas using language appropriate to audience, purpose 
and form
