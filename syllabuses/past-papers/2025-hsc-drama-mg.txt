Section I — Australian Drama and Theatre (Core Study) 
Question 1 
Criteria Marks 
• Explains insightfully how Australian plays could be staged to share 
diverse concerns with audiences 
• Provides insightful and relevant supporting evidence*, including reference to TWO plays in this topic 
• Provides a sustained, logical and cohesive response 17–20 
• Explains substantially how Australian plays could be staged to share diverse concerns with audiences 
• Provides relevant supporting evidence*, including reference to TWO plays in this topic 
• Provides a logical and cohesive response 13–16 
• Describes broadly how Australian plays could be staged to share diverse 
concerns with audiences 
• Provides some supporting evidence*, including reference to TWO plays 
in this topic 
• Provides a clear response 9–12 
• Outlines some aspects of the staging of diverse concerns and/or limited 
reference to sharing with audiences , with some reference to the play(s) 
• Provides a basic response with limited supporting evidence* 5–8 
• Comments on some ideas that relate to some aspects of the question 
• Provides a limited response with little or no supporting evidence* 1–4 
* Supporting evidence may include examples, quotations, evidence from the chosen text, real or imagined productions and/or practical experiences related to the selected topic area.
