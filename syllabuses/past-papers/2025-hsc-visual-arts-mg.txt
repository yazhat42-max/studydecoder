Sample answer: 
Plate 1 is a large acrylic painting depicting a group of figures dancing in the foreground. The 
full moon in the background rising over the sea illuminates the scene. The palette is muted 
with a blue cast, contrasted with some strong yellows in the clothin g. Several groups are 
dancing including couples, a trio of women (possibly a mother, daughter and granddaughter) 
and one woman dancing alone. The lone figure on the left is larger than the others and 
engages directly with the viewer. The stylised features and proportions of the figures enhance the dreamlike quality of the image. The rhythmic movement of the figures and the 
swirling skirts suggests the action of the dance and contrasts the stillness of the setting. 
Movement is further enhanced through the use of paintery b rushstrokes.

known to the artist

•  Immersive experience for the audience who can walk around and inside the work  
•  Provi

•  Space  engages the audience and invites them t  o think on the  importance  of  seed  
preservation and ecology  
•  Uses  new technologies  such  as  fibre optics  
•  Kinetic  element  of  the  fibre- optic cables connects with the idea of  seed dispersal

fragility of ecosystems and the importance of seed preservation

of  respecting our  natural  environment  
•  As  an  Expo  pavilion  the  purpose  is  to  showcase  innovation  and  educate  audiences  about  
contemporary issues on a global  stage .
